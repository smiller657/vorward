What is HEMA? Historical European Martial Arts (HEMA) academic study of ancient fight manuals or fechtbucher (fight-books) and the practice of recreating and learning the skills of the ancient European fight masters. Tournaments offer a way for practioners to meet, take classes, and test their skills in the art of combat.

Project Background: This project grew from a recognizable need for better software in the HEMA community. Maintaining fight records and tournament matches using Microsoft Excel and forms is slowing becoming a method of the past as interest in HEMA continues to grow.
